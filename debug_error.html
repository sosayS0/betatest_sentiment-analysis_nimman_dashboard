Taste & Experience Notes
Nov 2025 • Solo
Khao Soi Super Bowl: A really good meal. The creamy portion of curry juice and the tissue contrast left by the crispy noodle oil created a perfect balance. Especially the aroma of the broth—heavy but not heavy, layered. What was most surprised was the aroma of lemongrass and limestone in Sai Ua. The spice is strong yet clean, but also greases the curry juice and creates a sweet-spicy balance. The octopus and shrimp were unexpected but delightful details; the salty shrimp of seafood keeps the curry lively. Pairing with Chang’s beer was perfect—every sip refreshed the intensity of the curry.
